Toyota and Lexus Consulting Project

Tableau | Data Visualization, Data Cleaining, Market Research, Forecasting

Project Completed by: Jackson Bronkema and Pavan Ghantasala

Problem Statement

In our Data Visualization class, we were tasked with using monthly sales data sets and other data found online to present a marketing strategy that would increase the sales of both Toyota and Lexus. This project was challenging, but very exciting! Based on the data, we suggested that Toyota focus on selling SUV's, segement certain groups with certain cars, and increase their online marketing precense for hybrids and electric vehicles.
