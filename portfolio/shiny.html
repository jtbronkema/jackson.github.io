Problem Statement

The sports gambling industry has seen a rapid ascent in recent years. This is evident by the fact that there has been a large increase in the amount of money spent on sports gambling and the number of people who participate in sports betting both online and in casinos in recent years. With this knowledge, my group and I decided to create an all inclusive sports betting information hub in our Using R for Analytics class. We used the Shiny package through R to create this application. Some of the features of our dashboard include current twitter feeds with up-to-date information on the NBA, recent team and player statistics, a player analytics profile, player clustering analysis, and a single game predictor model with a probability of the winner.

Functionality

The dashboard consists of 5 pages. The first page includes hype video and informs the viewer on the NBA. The second page is the Team Profile. The team profile page gives team statistics, player statistics, and salary information for each NBA team. The players profile page allows users to compare the worth of different NBA players in a one-vs-one manner using analytical measures. In the fourth page of the application, we have cluster analysis. This aspect of the application allows users to cluster all current NBA players based on their total worth to a team. This is a great application for NBA executives or fantasy basketball players to use to pick the NBA players to their team that truly add the most value to their teams. The most importatnt aspect of our application is in the final page. This is a game prediction tool. For any possible match-up of NBA teams, this application uses logistic regression to predict which team will win and it also gives the probability that that team will win.
